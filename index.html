Peoplefy Team

Peoplefy is managed by a team of professionals who bring in rich experience in multiple aspects of business operations in Strategic Talent Management, Technology and Human Resource domain. The senior management at Peoplefy comes with global experience in end-to-end Human Resource Acquisition, Development and Management using Technology and Tools.

Managing Director:

The Peoplefy team is led by Mr. Rajesh Bharatiya who serves as the Managing Director of the company. Rajesh is responsible for formulating the company's overall strategy, vision and focus necessary for Peoplefy to continue it’s pace of rapid growth and expansion.

Previously, as Head of Human Resources at a software firm (Vertex Software), Rajesh contributed to the complete range of Strategic and Operational HR activities such as Talent Acquisition, Compensations & Benefits, Training & Development, Performance Appraisals, Employee Engagement, etc.

Prior to this, Rajesh worked in Global Software Product & Services organizations in the United States of America (Peopleclick Inc.) developing products for Human Resource Management. During this time, Rajesh played a key role in executing large & complex multi country Talent Management product implementation projects across USA, Canada & Europe.

Subject Matter Experts

Every recruitment associate at Peoplefy comes either with in-depth experience in Talent Acquisition (Executive Search and Selection) methods or undergoes thorough training at Peoplefy. Each team, in an industry vertical that we serve, is headed by a Subject Matter Expert (SME) who brings in relevant domain knowledge and experience to the talent search process.
